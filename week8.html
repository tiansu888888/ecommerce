Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. Under Kant's Categorical Imperative, how should a firm evaluate an ethical decision? 根据康德的绝对命令，企业应该如何评估伦理决策？

Explanation: Kant's Categorical Imperative states that if an action is not right for everyone to do, it is not right for anyone. 解析：康德的绝对命令指出，如果一个行为不适合所有人去做，那它就不适合任何人。

2. Which ethical principle advises taking the action that achieves the greatest net benefit for society? 哪种伦理学原理建议采取能够为社会带来最大净收益的行动？

Explanation: The Utilitarian Principle states that we should perform actions that result in the greatest total happiness/benefit. 解析：功利主义原理指出，我们应该执行能够带来最大总幸福/收益的行动。

3. GDPR is a privacy regulation framework enacted by which region? GDPR 是哪个地区颁布的隐私监管框架？

Explanation: GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) is a strict privacy regulation framework in the European Union. 解析：GDPR（通用数据保护条例）是欧盟严格的隐私监管框架。

4. Which intellectual property category protects intangible literary and artistic assets (e.g., photos, livestream videos)? 哪种知识产权类别保护无形的文学和艺术资产（例如照片、直播视频）？

Explanation: Copyright protects expression of ideas, including text, media, videos, and product design assets. 解析：著作权（版权）保护思想的表达，包括文本、媒体、视频和产品设计资产。

5. What is the primary function of a Trademark? 商标（Trademark）的主要功能是什么？

Explanation: A trademark is a recognizable sign, design, or expression which identifies products or services of a particular source. 解析：商标是识别特定来源的产品或服务的可识别标志、设计或表达。

6. A patent protects which of the following? 专利保护以下哪项？

Explanation: A patent grants exclusive rights for an invention, which is a product or a process that provides a new way of doing something. 解析：专利授予一项发明的专有权，该发明是提供一种做某事的新方法的产品或工艺。

7. Which data regulation law governs personal data protection specifically in Malaysia? 哪项数据监管法律专门管辖马来西亚的个人数据保护？

Explanation: In Malaysia, personal data in commercial transactions is governed by the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010. 解析：在马来西亚，商业交易中的个人数据受 2010 年《个人数据保护法》(PDPA) 管辖。

8. What is the primary ethical issue surrounding 'Platform Accountability'? 围绕“平台问责制”的主要伦理问题是什么？

Explanation: Platform accountability deals with whether platforms act as neutral hosts or exert editorial/operational control, making them liable. 解析：平台问责制涉及平台是作为中立主机运行，还是施加编辑/运营控制，从而使其承担责任。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Explain the four ethical dimensions of e-commerce: Information Rights, Property Rights, Governance, and Public Safety. 解释电子商务的四个伦理维度：信息权、财产权、治理和公共安全。

The four main dimensions are:
1. Information Rights (信息权): What rights to personal privacy do individuals possess? What data can platforms collect and scan?
2. Property Rights (财产权): How can intellectual property (copyrights, patents, trademarks) be protected in the digital space where replication is cheap?
3. Governance (治理): Who controls e-commerce platforms? How should digital transactions be taxed and regulated across national lines?
4. Public Safety (公共安全): How do we prevent access to illegal materials and protect vulnerable demographics online? 四个主要维度是：
1. 信息权：个人拥有哪些个人隐私权？平台可以收集和扫描哪些数据？
2. 财产权：在复制成本低廉的数字空间中，如何保护知识产权（著作权、专利、商标）？
3. 治理：谁控制电子商务平台？如何跨国界对数字交易进行征税和监管？
4. 公共安全：我们如何防止获取非法材料并保护在线的弱势群体？
Review Exercise 2 复习练习题 2 ▶

Analyze the ShopVibe scenario under Information Rights, Intellectual Property, and Platform Accountability. Apply Kantian and Utilitarian principles. 分析 ShopVibe 案例（信息权、知识产权和平台问责制）。应用康德和功利主义原理。

1. Information Rights (2 Marks): ShopVibe violated the independent merchants' privacy by silently scanning private DMs and transaction histories without consent, using this data for internal competitor brand analytics.
2. Intellectual Property (2 Marks): Buried ToS co-ownership clauses do not justify copyright plagiarism of Elena's signature design assets. The data breach leaking proprietary specifications represents a trade secret violation.
3. Accountability & Control (3 Marks): ShopVibe's neutral host defense is invalid because they active-copied designs and placed cheap copies at the top of search listings, exerting direct market control.
4. Ethical Principles (3 Marks):
- Kantian (绝对命令): Action fails. Scanning DMs and plagiarizing designs cannot be universalized (no business would transact under those terms).
- Utilitarian (功利主义): Action fails. Maximized benefit for one firm at the cost of massive negative utility for creators and overall consumer trust. 1. 信息权（2分）：ShopVibe 在未经同意的情况下默默扫描私人信件和交易历史记录，并将此数据用于内部竞争品牌分析，侵犯了独立商家的隐私。
2. 知识产权（2分）：隐蔽的 ToS 共同所有权条款不能合理化对 Elena 签名设计资产的著作权抄袭。泄露专有规格的数据泄露代表了对商业秘密的侵犯。
3. 问责制与控制（3分）：ShopVibe 的中立主机辩护是无效的，因为他们主动抄袭设计，并将廉价的复制品放在搜索列表的顶部，施加了直接的市场控制。
4. 伦理原则（3分）：
- 康德绝对命令：失败。扫描信件和剽窃设计无法被普遍化（没有任何企业会在此类条款下进行交易）。
- 功利主义：失败。以创作者的巨大负效用和整体消费者信任为代价，最大化了单个企业的利益。
