Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. Which paywall system allows users to view a limited number of articles before locking content? 哪种付费墙系统允许用户在锁定内容前查看有限数量的文章？

Explanation: Metered paywalls allow a specific free quota of articles (e.g., 3-5 articles) before demanding subscription credentials. 解析：计量付费墙在要求订阅凭证前允许特定数量的免费文章配额（如 3-5 篇）。

2. Digital Rights Management (DRM) is primarily used to __________________. 数字版权管理（DRM）主要用于 __________________。

Explanation: DRM consists of software and cryptographic solutions designed to protect digital media assets from piracy. 解析：DRM 由旨在保护数字媒体资产免受盗版的软件和加密解决方案组成。

3. Which convergence dimension refers to print, audio, and video content merging into a single digital platform? 哪种融合维度是指印刷、音频和视频内容融合到单个数字平台中？

Explanation: Content convergence refers to the integration of various media formats (text, audio, video) into a single digital content stream. 解析：内容融合是指将各种媒体格式（文本、音频、视频）整合到单个数字内容流中。

4. A hard paywall __________________. 硬付费墙 __________________。

Explanation: A hard paywall requires immediate subscription; no article content is accessible to non-paying users. 解析：硬付费墙要求立即订阅；非付费用户无法访问任何文章内容。

5. In the AIDA copywriting framework, what does the first 'A' stand for? 在 AIDA 文案框架中，第一个“A”代表什么？

Explanation: AIDA stands for Attention, Interest, Desire, Action. The first stage is grabbing the reader's attention. 解析：AIDA 代表注意、兴趣、欲望、行动。第一阶段是吸引读者的注意力。

6. The Spotify business model is best described as an example of: Spotify 的商业模式最适合被描述为以下哪项的示例：

Explanation: Spotify provides unlimited access to its entire music streaming catalog for a flat monthly subscription fee. 解析：Spotify 允许以定期的月度订阅费无限访问其整个音乐流媒体目录。

7. Digital media convergence allows content to be delivered across __________________. 数字媒体融合允许将内容分发到 __________________。

Explanation: Media convergence merges content formats so they can be parsed by any computing client device. 解析：媒体融合合并了内容格式，以便可以通过任何计算客户端设备对其进行解析。

8. What is a metered paywall's main benefit compared to a hard paywall? 与硬付费墙相比，计量付费墙的主要好处是什么？

Explanation: Metered paywalls allow search crawlers to index content and let users sample articles, building audience trust. 解析：计量付费墙允许搜索爬虫对内容进行索引，并让用户对文章进行采样，从而建立受众信任。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Explain the concept of media convergence. Identify the three main dimensions of convergence. 解释媒体融合（Media Convergence）的概念。指出媒体融合的三个主要维度。

1. Concept (概念): Media convergence refers to the merging of previously distinct media technologies and content formats (text, audio, video) into a single digital infrastructure and client device.
2. Three Dimensions (三个维度):
- Technology Convergence: Standardizing hardware and communication channels to run digital media.
- Content Convergence: Transforming analog print, sound, and video into digital streams that can be shared and co-created.
- Industry Convergence: Merging of media publishing firms with telecommunications and computing companies (e.g. Disney buying streaming services). 1. 概念：媒体融合是指将以前不同的媒体技术和内容格式（文本、音频、视频）合并到单一的数字基础设施和客户端设备中。
2. 三个维度：
- 技术融合：标准化硬件和通信渠道以运行数字媒体。
- 内容融合：将模拟的印刷、声音和视频转化为可以共享和共同创作的数字流。
- 行业融合：媒体出版公司与电信和计算机公司的合并（如迪士尼购买流媒体服务）。
