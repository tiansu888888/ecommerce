Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. What is the primary function of Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) in B2B transactions? 电子数据交换（EDI）在 B2B 交易中的主要功能是什么？

Explanation: EDI is the computer-to-computer exchange of business documents (invoices, purchase orders) in standard formats. 解析：EDI 是商业文档（如发票、采购单）在计算机之间以标准格式进行的直接交换。

2. A B2B procurement process starts with which of the following activities? B2B 采购流程始于以下哪项活动？

Explanation: The procurement cycle begins with searching for vendors, negotiating terms, and issuing requests for proposals (RFPs). 解析：采购周期始于寻找供应商、谈判条款并发布提案请求书 (RFP)。

3. The buying-side B2B marketplace is owned primarily by __________________. 买方 B2B 市场主要由 __________________ 所有。

Explanation: Buying-side marketplaces are set up by large buyers to invite bidding quotes and manage procurement from multiple suppliers. 解析：买方市场由大买家设立，用以邀请竞价报价并管理来自多个供应商的采购。

4. Which of the following describes collaborative commerce? 以下哪项描述了协同商务（Collaborative commerce）？

Explanation: Collaborative commerce uses digital networks to coordinate design, supply levels, and logistics between business partners. 解析：协同商务使用数字网络来协调业务合作伙伴之间的设计、供应水平和物流。

5. What does supply lead time measure? 供应提前期测量的是什么？

Explanation: Supply lead time is the elapsed time from placing a procurement order to receiving the cargo at the warehouse. 解析：供应提前期是指从下达采购订单到仓库收到货物所经过的时间。

6. Which B2B system focuses on coordinating delivery schedules and raw material stocks across platforms? 哪个 B2B 系统侧重于跨平台协调交货时间表和原材料库存？

Explanation: SCM systems coordinate the flows of materials, information, and finance along the supply chain network. 解析：SCM 系统协调供应链网络中物流、信息流和资金流的运转。

7. The selling-side B2B marketplace is best illustrated by which example? 卖方 B2B 市场最适合用哪个例子来解释？

Explanation: Selling-side portals are set up by a single major supplier (like Cisco or Intel) to sell to multiple business buyers. 解析：卖方门户由单个主要供应商（如思科或英特尔）设立，以向多个企业买家进行销售。

8. Why is SCM integration difficult in global B2B commerce? 为什么 SCM 整合在全球 B2B 商务中较为困难？

Explanation: Global supply chains face complexities including international border delays, differing PDPA/GDPR compliance, and timezone differences. 解析：全球供应链面临复杂的因素，包括国际边境延迟、不同的 PDPA/GDPR 合规性以及时区差异。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Explain B2B e-commerce models and supply chain management. Outline the procurement steps and key SCM metrics. 解释 B2B 电子商务模式和供应链管理。概述采购步骤和关键 SCM 指标。

1. B2B Models (B2B 模式): Facilitate commercial transactions between businesses. SCM coordinates materials and logistics.
2. Procurement Steps (采购步骤):
Step 1: Search and identify potential suppliers.
Step 2: Qualify vendors and issue Request for Proposal (RFP).
Step 3: Negotiate prices, credit terms, and issue Purchase Orders.
Step 4: Receive invoice via EDI, process receipt of goods, and clear payment.
3. Key SCM Metrics (SCM 指标): Lead time (order delay), inventory turnover (sales speed), and supply chain cost. 1. B2B 模式：促进企业之间的商业交易。SCM 协调物料和物流。
2. 采购步骤：
步骤 1：寻找并识别潜在供应商。
步骤 2：对供应商进行资格审查并发布提案请求书 (RFP)。
步骤 3：谈判价格、信用条款并发布采购订单。
步骤 4：通过 EDI 接收发票，处理收货并结清付款。
3. 关键 SCM 指标：提前期（订单延迟）、库存周转率（销售速度）和供应链成本。
