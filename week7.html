Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. Geofencing triggers smartphone advertising alerts based on which technology? 地理围栏（Geofencing）基于哪种技术触发智能手机广告警报？

Explanation: Geofencing defines a virtual perimeter around a physical location using GPS coordinates, triggering alerts when devices cross it. 解析：地理围栏使用 GPS 坐标在物理位置周围定义虚拟边界，在设备越过边界时触发警报。

2. Which of the following is a primary metric of engagement in social marketing? 以下哪项是社交营销中互动参与（Engagement）的主要指标？

Explanation: Engagement measures the active social interactions (likes, shares, comments) users have with brand content. 解析：互动参与度测量用户与品牌内容的主动社交互动（点赞、分享、评论）。

3. Local marketing primarily targets consumers based on __________________. 本地营销主要根据 __________________ 锁定消费者。

Explanation: Local marketing is targeted to consumers in a specific geographical area or near their current physical location. 解析：本地营销针对特定地理区域或当前物理位置附近的消费者进行定位。

4. Mobile marketing is critical in e-commerce because __________________. 移动营销在电子商务中至关重要，因为 __________________。

Explanation: Mobile marketing leverages the ubiquitous nature of smartphones, allowing real-time context-specific targeting. 解析：移动营销利用了智能手机无处不在的特性，允许进行实时的、基于特定上下文的定位。

5. The social graph is a digital map of __________________. 社交图谱是 __________________ 的数字图谱。

Explanation: The social graph represents the social connections and relationships between users on internet platforms. 解析：社交图谱表示互联网平台上用户之间的社交连接和关系。

6. What is location-based marketing? 什么是基于位置的营销？

Explanation: Location-based marketing relies on geolocational data (GPS/Wi-Fi) to target customers within physical proximity. 解析：基于位置的营销依赖地理位置数据（GPS/Wi-Fi）来针对物理上邻近的客户。

7. Organic reach in social media refers to content shown to users __________________. 社交媒体中的自然触达（Organic reach）是指向用户显示的内容 __________________。

Explanation: Organic reach is the number of unique users who see your posts without any paid promotion or ads. 解析：自然触达是指在没有任何付费推广或广告的情况下看到您帖子的唯一用户数。

8. Which mobile marketing format targets customers based on local directory searches? 哪种移动营销格式根据本地目录搜索来针对客户？

Explanation: Local search ads show up when users look for local business services on search tools (like Google Maps). 解析：当用户在搜索工具（如谷歌地图）上寻找本地商业服务时，会显示本地搜索广告。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Describe the Social Marketing process and identify key metrics used to measure Facebook campaign performance. 描述社交营销（Social Marketing）流程，并指出用于衡量 Facebook 广告系列表现的关键指标。

1. Social Marketing Process: Follows five key steps: Fan Acquisition (attracting users), Engagement (getting interaction), Amplification (sharing content), Community building (forums/pages), and Brand Strength (conversions/sales).
2. Performance Metrics (表现指标):
- Fan Acquisition: Number of page likes, followers, and organic impressions.
- Engagement: Likes, shares, comment counts, and Click-Through Rate (CTR) on posts.
- Amplification: Number of retweets, shares, and referrals driven by users. 1. 社交营销流程：遵循五个关键步骤：粉丝获取（吸引用户）、互动参与（进行互动）、放大传播（共享内容）、社区建设（论坛/主页）和品牌实力（转化/销售）。
2. 表现指标：
- 粉丝获取：主页点赞数、关注者和自然展示次数。
- 互动参与：帖子上的点赞数、分享数、评论数以及点击率 (CTR)。
- 放大传播：用户带来的转发、分享和推介次数。
