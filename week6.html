Practical Exercise: Calculating CPM, CPC, and CTR 实践练习：计算 CPM、CPC 与 CTR

Steps to complete:

  1. Assume a marketing budget of $500.
  2. If the campaign gets 50,000 impressions and 1,000 clicks, calculate the CTR (Clicks/Impressions) and CPC (Cost/Clicks).
  3. Solve for the CPM (Cost/Impressions * 1000) of this campaign.

操作步骤：

  1. 假设营销预算为 500 美元。
  2. 如果该广告系列获得 50,000 次展示和 1,000 次点击，计算点击率 CTR (Clicks/Impressions) 和每次点击成本 CPC (Cost/Clicks)。
  3. 计算此广告系列的每千次展示成本 CPM (Cost/Impressions * 1000)。
Click-Through Rate (CTR) = 1,000 / 50,000 = 2.0%\nCost Per Click (CPC) = $500 / 1,000 = $0.50\nCost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM) = ($500 / 50,000) * 1,000 = $10.00

Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. What does the acronym 'SEO' stand for in digital marketing? 数字营销中的“SEO”代表什么？

Explanation: SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, which focuses on gaining organic search result placement. 解析：SEO 代表搜索引擎优化，侧重于获取自然搜索结果位置。

2. If a merchant pays $200 for a banner campaign that generates 20,000 impressions, what is the CPM? 如果商家为产生 20,000 次展示的横幅广告系列支付 200 美元，CPM 是多少？

Explanation: CPM = (Cost / Impressions) * 1000 = ($200 / 20,000) * 1000 = $10.00. 解析：CPM = (成本 / 展示量) * 1000 = ($200 / 20,000) * 1000 = $10.00。

3. Click-Through Rate (CTR) is calculated as __________________. 点击率 (CTR) 的计算方式为 __________________。

Explanation: CTR is the ratio of users who click on a specific link to the number of total users who view the page/ad (impressions). 解析：CTR 是点击特定链接的用户数与查看页面/广告的总用户数（展示数）的比率。

4. Which tracking technology is placed on client browsers to identify returning website users? 哪种追踪技术被放置在客户端浏览器上以识别返回网站的用户？

Explanation: Cookies are small text files stored by browsers to persist session identifiers and track user navigation history. 解析：Cookie 是浏览器存储的小文本文件，用于持久化会话标识符并追踪用户导航历史。

5. If a campaign costs $300 and drives 600 active clicks, what is the CPC? 如果一个广告系列花费 300 美元并带来 600 次点击，CPC 是多少？

Explanation: CPC = Cost / Clicks = $300 / 600 = $0.50. 解析：CPC = 成本 / 点击数 = $300 / 600 = $0.50。

6. What is organic search traffic? 什么是自然搜索流量？

Explanation: Organic traffic is unpaid traffic arriving at a website through search engines, which is optimized using SEO. 解析：自然流量是指通过搜索引擎到达网站的未付费流量，这可以通过 SEO 进行优化。

7. Which metric focuses purely on direct transactions and acquisitions divided by cost? 哪个指标纯粹关注直接交易数和获取数除以成本？

Explanation: CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) determines the direct average cost of a customer conversion transaction. 解析：CPA（每次获取成本）确定了客户转化交易的直接平均成本。

8. Search Engine Marketing (SEM) differs from SEO in that SEM: 搜索引擎营销 (SEM) 与 SEO 的不同之处在于 SEM：

Explanation: SEM involves paying for search placement using keyword bidding tools, whereas SEO is organic. 解析：SEM 涉及使用关键词竞价工具付费获取搜索排名，而 SEO 是自然排名的。

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

A business runs an online marketing campaign with a budget of $1200. The campaign generates 60,000 impressions, resulting in 1,500 clicks and 75 final purchase conversions. Calculate the CTR, CPC, and CPA for this campaign. 某企业运行在线营销，预算为 1200 美元。该活动产生 60,000 次展示，带来 1,500 次点击和 75 次最终购买转化。计算此营销的 CTR、CPC 和 CPA。

1. Click-Through Rate (CTR) Calculation:
Formula: (Clicks / Impressions) * 100 = (1,500 / 60,000) * 100 = 2.50% (1 Mark)
2. Cost Per Click (CPC) Calculation:
Formula: Total Cost / Clicks = $1,200 / 1,500 = $0.80 (1 Mark)
3. Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) Calculation:
Formula: Total Cost / Conversions = $1,200 / 75 = $16.00 (1 Mark) 1. 点击率 (CTR) 计算：
公式：（点击数 / 展示数）* 100 = (1,500 / 60,000) * 100 = 2.50%（1分）
2. 每次点击成本 (CPC) 计算：
公式：总成本 / 点击数 = $1,200 / 1,500 = $0.80（1分）
3. 每次获取成本 (CPA) 计算：
公式：总成本 / 转化数 = $1,200 / 75 = $16.00（1分）
