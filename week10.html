Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. The online service sector differs from the physical retail sector in that services are: 在线服务行业与实体零售行业的不同之处在于，服务是：

Explanation: Services are intangible processes and activities, which cannot be stored or touched physically. 解析：服务是无形的过程和活动，不能以物理方式存储或触摸。

2. Which model executes financial transactions for users online, saving time? 哪种模式在线为用户执行金融交易，从而节省时间？

Explanation: A transaction broker executes user trades or travel bookings (e.g. PayPal, Expedia) online. 解析：交易经纪人在网上执行用户交易或旅游预订（例如 PayPal、Expedia）。

3. Which of the following represents an online transaction broker? 以下哪项代表在线交易经纪人？

Explanation: Expedia is an online travel transaction broker that processes flight/hotel reservations for consumers. 解析：Expedia 是一家在线旅游交易经纪商，为消费者处理机票/酒店预订。

4. What does checking the 'Virtual' box in WooCommerce product settings do? 在 WooCommerce 产品设置中勾选“虚拟 (Virtual)”复选框有什么作用？

Explanation: Virtual products are intangible; checking the box disables all shipping configurations and checkout shipping forms. 解析：虚拟产品是无形的；勾选该框将禁用所有运输配置和结账运输表单。

5. Which service industry has seen the highest digital penetration and displacement of physical storefronts? 哪个服务行业的数字渗透率最高且取代了实体店面？

Explanation: Online banking and travel booking have migrated almost entirely online, displacing traditional brick-and-mortar storefronts. 解析：在线银行和旅游预订已几乎完全迁移到网上，取代了传统的实体店面。

6. What is the primary revenue source for financial portals? 金融门户网站的主要收入来源是什么？

Explanation: Financial portals provide news and advice, monetizing via display advertising and affiliate referrals to brokerages. 解析：金融门户提供新闻和建议，通过展示广告和向经纪人进行联属引流来实现商业变现。

7. Offline service providers are limited primarily by __________________. 线下服务提供商主要受限于 __________________。

Explanation: Traditional offline services are constrained by local store boundaries and business operating hours. 解析：传统的线下服务受限于当地商店边界和业务营业时间。

8. Why is security critical in online financial services compared to traditional brick-and-mortar banking? 与传统的实体银行相比，为什么安全在在线金融服务中至关重要？

Explanation: Online financial systems are target vectors for social engineering (phishing) and credential sniffing, demanding robust security. 解析：在线金融系统是社会工程（网络钓鱼）和凭证嗅探的目标载体，因此需要强大的安全保护。

Differentiate between Transaction Brokers and Market Creators in the online service sector, giving business examples. 区分在线服务行业中的交易经纪人与市场创建者，并给出商业示例。

Example: E*Trade (stock brokerage) or Expedia (travel booking). (1 Mark)
2. Market Creator (市场创建者): Builds a digital environment where buyers and sellers meet, display products, and negotiate prices.

示例：E*Trade（股票经纪）或 Expedia（旅游预订）。（1分）
