Practical Exercise: Drafting a Value Proposition 实践练习：撰写价值主张

Steps to complete:

  1. Identify a target market segment (e.g. busy college students).
  2. Define the pain point (e.g. no time to shop for fresh groceries).

Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. Which business model builds a digital environment where buyers and sellers can meet, display products, and establish prices? 哪种商业模式构建了一个买家和卖家可以会面、展示产品和确定价格的数字环境？

Explanation: A market creator builds a digital environment where buyers and sellers meet and execute transactions (e.g. eBay, Etsy). 解析：市场创建者构建了一个买家和卖家会面并执行交易的数字环境（如 eBay、Etsy）。

2. Facebook relies primarily on which of the following revenue models? 脸书（Facebook）主要依赖以下哪种收入模式？

Explanation: Facebook's primary business revenue comes from showing advertising to its massive user base. 解析：Facebook 的主要业务收入来自向其庞大的用户群展示广告。

3. What is the key challenge associated with the freemium revenue strategy? 与免费增值收入战略相关的关键挑战是什么？

Explanation: The key challenge of freemium is converting a sufficient percentage of free users to paying ones to cover the costs of the free tier. 解析：免费增值的关键挑战是将足够比例的免费用户转化为付费用户，以抵消免费层级的成本。

4. Which model involves a business processing transactions for consumers, saving them time and money? 哪种模式涉及企业为消费者处理交易，从而为他们节省时间和金钱？

Explanation: A transaction broker executes online transactions for consumers (e.g., stock brokerages, travel agents, PayPal), collecting fees. 解析：交易经纪人为消费者执行在线交易（例如股票经纪、旅行社、PayPal），收取费用。

5. Which B2B business model acts as a digital marketplace connecting many buyers with many sellers? 哪种 B2B 商业模式充当了连接众多买家与众多卖家的数字市场？

Explanation: Net marketplaces (or e-hubs) are B2B digital hubs where multiple buying businesses transact with multiple sellers. 解析：净市场（或电子枢纽）是 B2B 数字中心，多个买方企业在此与多个卖方进行交易。

6. A value proposition defines: 价值主张定义了：

Explanation: The value proposition describes how the firm's product solves customer problems and why they should choose your brand. 解析：价值主张描述了公司的产品如何解决客户问题，以及他们为什么要选择你的品牌。

7. Which of the following is NOT one of the five basic revenue models? 以下哪项不是五种基本的收入模式之一？

Explanation: Venture capital is a source of investment funding, not a recurring customer-facing revenue model. 解析：风险投资是投资资金来源，而不是面向客户的循环收入模式。

8. What is first-mover advantage? 什么是先发优势？

Explanation: First-mover advantage is the competitive advantage gained by the first significant occupant of a market segment. 解析：先发优势是指细分市场中首个重要进入者所获得的竞争优势。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Describe the Affiliate Marketing and Subscription Revenue models, providing real-world business examples. 描述联属营销和订阅收入模式，并提供真实的商业示例。

1. Affiliate Revenue Model (联属收入模式): A business steers web traffic to an affiliate's website. In return, the steering site receives a referral fee or a percentage of the revenue from any resulting sales.
Example: The Amazon Associates program where bloggers link to book listings and earn commissions.
2. Subscription Revenue Model (订阅收入模式): A website offers content or services to users for a recurring, periodic fee (e.g. monthly or annually).
Example: Netflix charging monthly subscriptions to stream video catalog. 1. 联属收入模式：企业将网络流量引向联属伙伴的网站。作为回报，引流网站将获得推介费或任何交易额的百分比分成。
示例：亚马逊联属计划 (Amazon Associates)，博主链接到图书页面并赚取佣金。
2. 订阅收入模式：网站向用户提供内容或服务，并收取定期、循环的费用（如按月或按年）。
示例：Netflix 每月收取订阅费以在线播放视频目录。
Review Exercise 2 复习练习题 2 ▶

Discuss how e-commerce platforms can establish a Sustainable Competitive Advantage in a crowded digital marketplace. 讨论电子商务平台如何在拥挤的数字市场中建立可持续的竞争优势。

An e-commerce firm can achieve a sustainable competitive advantage through:
1. Proprietary Technology: Unique search algorithms or automation integrations that are hard to replicate.
2. Customer Lock-in: Developing a high switching cost ecosystem (e.g., Apple iOS, customized databases).
3. Brand Equity: Establishing organic consumer trust and a strong reputation.
4. Supply Chain Efficiency: Low cost fulfillment operations (e.g. Amazon Prime networks) permitting lower sales margins than competitors. 电子商务企业可以通过以下方式获得可持续的竞争优势：
1. 专有技术：难以复制的独特搜索算法或自动化集成。
2. 客户锁定：开发高切换成本的生态系统（如苹果 iOS、定制数据库）。
3. 品牌资产：建立消费者有机的信任和强大的声誉。
4. 供应链效率：低成本的物流配送业务（如亚马逊 Prime 网络），允许比竞争对手更低的销售利润率。
