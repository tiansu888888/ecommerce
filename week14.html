Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. Private industrial networks represent __________________. 私有工业网络代表 __________________。

Explanation: Private industrial networks are closed, invite-only B2B networks owned by a major firm to coordinate with trusted suppliers. 解析：私有工业网络是由主要公司拥有的闭环、仅限邀请的 B2B 网络，用于与受信任的供应商进行协调。

2. Which of the following is a key characteristic of Net Marketplaces? 以下哪项是净市场（Net Marketplaces）的关键特征？

Explanation: Net marketplaces are open digital e-hubs that connect many buying businesses with multiple selling suppliers. 解析：净市场是开放式的数字电子枢纽，连接众多买方企业与多家销售供应商。

3. An industry consortium is a B2B net marketplace sponsored by __________________. 行业联盟（Industry consortium）是由 __________________ 赞助的 B2B 净市场。

Explanation: Industry consortia are B2B hubs owned and sponsored by a group of leading buyer corporations in a specific industry. 解析：行业联盟是由特定行业内的一组领先买方企业所有并赞助的 B2B 枢纽。

4. A B2B e-distributor sells __________________. B2B 电子分销商销售 __________________。

Explanation: E-distributors sell indirect goods (MRO supplies) directly to other business clients (e.g. Grainger). 解析：电子分销商直接向其他企业客户销售间接商品（MRO 物资）（例如 Grainger）。

5. Which pricing strategy is commonly used in net marketplaces compared to private networks? 与私有网络相比，净市场中通常使用哪种定价策略？

Explanation: Net marketplaces often employ dynamic pricing mechanisms (bidding, catalogs, spot pricing), whereas private networks use contracted pricing. 解析：净市场通常采用动态定价机制（竞价、目录、现货价格），而私有网络使用合同定价。

6. Independent exchanges are owned by __________________. 独立交易所（Independent exchanges）由 __________________ 所有。

Explanation: Independent exchanges are B2B hubs owned by third-party intermediaries seeking transaction fee commissions. 解析：独立交易所是由寻求交易费佣金的第三方中介拥有的 B2B 枢纽。

7. Private industrial networks enable deep integration of which system? 私有工业网络实现了哪个系统的深度集成？

Explanation: Private networks enable deep integration of partner ERP, inventory database, and production planning databases. 解析：私有网络实现合作伙伴 ERP、库存数据库和生产计划数据库的深度集成。

8. A B2B checkout flow simulation verifies __________________. B2B 结账流程模拟验证了 __________________。

Explanation: Simulating the cart checkout flow ensures transaction databases update stock counts and verify processing logic. 解析：模拟购物车结账流程可确保交易数据库更新库存计数并验证处理逻辑。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Compare Net Marketplaces and Private Industrial Networks. Discuss how they differ in ownership, membership, and pricing. 对比净市场（Net Marketplaces）与私有工业网络。讨论它们在所有权、成员资格和定价方面的差异。

1. Ownership (所有权): Net marketplaces are owned by third-party intermediaries or buyer consortia. Private industrial networks are owned by a single dominant buyer or manufacturing alliance (1 Mark).
2. Membership (成员资格): Net marketplaces are open digital hubs connecting many buying businesses with multiple selling vendors. Private networks are closed, invite-only trusted supplier guilds (1 Mark).
3. Pricing (定价): Net marketplaces use dynamic pricing (auctions, catalogs, spot exchanges), while private networks use fixed contracted prices (1 Mark). 1. 所有权：净市场由第三方中介或买方联盟所有。私有工业网络由单个主导买方或制造联盟所有（1分）。
2. 成员资格：净市场是开放式的数字中心，连接众多买方企业与多家销售供应商。私有网络是闭环的、仅限邀请的受信任供应商协会（1分）。
3. 定价：净市场采用动态定价（拍卖、目录、现货交易），而私有网络使用固定的合同价格（1分）。
