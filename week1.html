Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. Which of the following is a unique feature of Electronic Commerce (E-commerce)? 以下哪项是电子商务（E-commerce）的独特特征？

Explanation: Ubiquity is one of the eight unique features of e-commerce technology. It refers to its availability everywhere at all times. 解析：“无处不在”是电子商务技术的八大独特特征之一。它指的是其随时随地可用。

2. What is the primary difference between E-commerce and E-business? 电子商务与电子业务之间的主要区别是什么？

Explanation: E-commerce focuses on transactions that cross organizational boundaries, whereas E-business is the digital enablement of internal processes. 解析：电子商务专注于跨越组织边界的交易，而电子业务是内部流程的数字化实现。

3. Which feature of e-commerce technology refers to the total amount and quality of information available to all market participants? 电子商务技术的哪项特征是指所有市场参与者可获得的信息总量和质量？

Explanation: Information density refers to the total amount and quality of information available to all market participants, consumers and merchants alike. 解析：信息密度是指所有市场参与者（消费者和商家）均可获得的信息总量和质量。

4. M-commerce (Mobile Commerce) involves the use of which device? 移动商务（M-commerce）涉及使用哪种设备？

Explanation: M-commerce stands for mobile commerce, which involves transactions using mobile wireless devices. 解析：M-commerce 代表移动商务，指使用移动无线设备进行的交易。

5. Which feature allows merchants to target their marketing messages to specific individuals based on name, interests, and past purchases? 哪项特征允许商家根据姓名、兴趣和过去的购买记录向特定个人发送有针对性的营销信息？

Explanation: Personalization allows merchants to target marketing messages to specific individuals based on demographic, behavioral, or other data. 解析：个性化允许商家根据人口统计学、行为或其他数据将营销信息定位到特定个人。

6. Universal standards in e-commerce refer to the fact that: 电子商务中的通用标准是指以下事实：

Explanation: Universal standards mean the technical standards of the Internet and e-commerce are shared by all nations, lowering market entry costs. 解析：通用标准意味着互联网和电子商务的技术标准由所有国家共享，降低了市场准入门槛。

7. What is the term used to describe the ability of consumers to discover the actual costs merchants pay for products? 用什么术语来描述消费者发现商家为产品支付的实际成本的能力？

Explanation: Cost transparency refers to the ability of consumers to find out the actual costs merchants pay for products, which is enhanced by e-commerce. 解析：成本透明度是指消费者找出商家为产品支付的实际成本的能力，这在电子商务中得到了增强。

8. Which academic discipline concerned with e-commerce focuses on consumer behavior and brand building? 涉及电子商务的哪门学术学科侧重于消费者行为和品牌建设？

Explanation: Marketing is a business/behavioral discipline concerned with consumer behavior, market segmenting, and brand positioning in e-commerce. 解析：市场营销是一门与电子商务中的消费者行为、市场细分和品牌定位相关的商业/行为学科。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Compare E-commerce and E-business in detail. Identify the primary boundary line between them. 详细对比电子商务与电子业务。指出它们之间的主要界限。

1. E-commerce (电子商务): Primarily involves commercial transactions conducted electronically across organizational boundaries. It includes exchange of value (transactions) between a firm and its customers, suppliers, or payment networks.
2. E-business (电子业务): Primarily involves the digital enablement of transactions and business processes within a firm (e.g. inventory control, intranet communications, processing of internal files).
Primary Boundary (边界线): The boundary line is the firm boundary. E-commerce transactions cross organization lines (outward-facing), while E-business processes are internally focused (inward-facing). 1. 电子商务 (E-commerce)：主要涉及跨越组织边界以电子方式进行的商业交易。它包括企业与其客户、供应商或支付网络之间的价值交换（交易）。
2. 电子业务 (E-business)：主要指企业内部交易和业务流程的数字化实现（如库存控制、内联网通信、内部文件处理）。
主要界限：分界线是企业边界。电子商务交易跨越组织边界（面向外部），而电子业务流程则聚焦于内部（面向内部）。
Review Exercise 2 复习练习题 2 ▶

Explain how Information Density and Price Transparency affect the competitive landscape in an e-commerce market. 解释信息密度和价格透明度如何影响电子商务市场中的竞争格局。

1. Information Density: E-commerce increases the total volume, quality, and accuracy of information available to all market participants. This reduces search costs and makes transaction information symmetric.
2. Price Transparency: It allows consumers to immediately view and compare prices across multiple competing online merchants.
Effect on Competition: High price transparency reduces merchants' ability to hide true costs and charge high markups based on consumer ignorance, intensifying price competition and driving down profit margins on commodity products. 1. 信息密度：电子商务增加了所有市场参与者可获得的信息总量、质量和准确性。这降低了搜索成本，使交易信息变得对称。
2. 价格透明度：允许消费者立即查看和比较多个竞争性在线商家的价格。
对竞争的影响：高价格透明度降低了商家隐瞒真实成本并利用消费者无知收取高额加价的能力，从而加剧了价格竞争并压低了商品化产品的利润率。
