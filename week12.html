Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. What is the primary characteristic of an online auction portal? 在线拍卖门户网站的主要特征是什么？

Explanation: Auctions use dynamic pricing, where the final price of an item is established through bidding in the marketspace. 解析：拍卖采用动态定价，即商品的最终价格是通过在市场空间中竞价确立的。

2. What is shill bidding in online auctions? 在线拍卖中的“托儿竞价”是指什么？

Explanation: Shill bidding is a type of auction fraud where the seller (or accomplices) places fake bids to drive the price up. 解析：托儿竞价是一种拍卖欺诈，指卖家（或同伙）投放虚假竞标以抬高价格。

3. Which business model generates revenue primarily through display ads and steering affiliate traffic? 哪种商业模式主要通过展示广告和引导联属流量来产生收入？

Explanation: Portals aggregate search, directories, and news, monetizing via ad impressions and referral affiliate commissions. 解析：门户网站聚合搜索、目录和新闻，通过广告展示和推荐联属佣金来实现商业变现。

4. What is bid rigging in auction portals? 拍卖门户网站中的“围标/恶意串通”是指什么？

Explanation: Bid rigging involves colluding bidders conspiring to limit bidding competition, depressing the final auction price. 解析：围标涉及串通的竞标者合谋限制竞价竞争，从而压低最终拍卖价格。

5. Which online community type organizes users around shared vocational values or professions? 哪种在线社区类型将用户围绕共享的职业价值或专业组织起来？

Explanation: Professional communities target specific career guilds, vocational interests, and industry collaboration. 解析：专业社区针对特定的职业协会、行业兴趣和行业协作。

6. Which auction type features a single seller and multiple bidding buyers competing upward? 哪种拍卖类型的特征是单个卖家和多个竞价买家向上竞争？

Explanation: The English auction is the most common format, where buyers place progressively higher bids until the timer expires. 解析：英式拍卖是最常见的格式，买家依次报出更高的价格，直至时间截止。

7. An online portal's success relies heavily on user __________________. 在线门户网站的成功高度依赖用户的 __________________。

Explanation: Stickiness measures the platform's ability to retain visitors, encouraging repeated return visits and higher ad exposures. 解析：粘性衡量平台留住访问者的能力，鼓励重复访问并提高广告曝光率。

8. Why are 'Verified Owner' labels important in WooCommerce reviews? 为什么“已验证的所有者”标签在 WooCommerce 评价中很重要？

Explanation: Verified owner labels establish trust by asserting that comment reviewers completed actual transactions for the item. 解析：“已验证的所有者”标签通过断定评论者完成了该商品的实际交易来建立信任。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Describe three common types of auction fraud. Outline rules portals can implement to mitigate these risks. 描述三种常见的拍卖欺诈类型。概述门户网站可以实施以减轻这些风险的规则。

1. Types of Fraud:
- Shill Bidding: Sellers use fake accounts to bid on their own listings to drive prices up.
- Bid Siphoning: Contacting bidders directly to pull them off the portal to transact privately.
- Non-Delivery: Collecting winning bid funds but failing to ship the purchased items.
2. Mitigation Rules (缓解规则):
- Implement strict identity verification checks (KYC) for sellers.
- Block bid entries from matching IP addresses or identical payment profiles as the seller.
- Enforce escrow secure payment solutions (e.g. holding funds until the buyer confirms delivery). 1. 欺诈类型：
- 托儿竞价：卖家使用虚假账号在自己发布商品中竞价，以抬高价格。
- 竞价引流：直接联系竞标者以使其离开门户进行私下交易。
- 不发货：收取赢得竞标的资金但未能寄送所购商品。
2. 缓解规则：
- 对卖家实施严格的身份验证检查 (KYC)。
- 阻止来自与卖家相匹配的 IP 地址或相同支付方式的竞价输入。
- 实施托管安全支付解决方案（如扣留资金直到买家确认送达）。
