Knowledge Check Practice 知识自测练习

1. A virtual merchant is best described as an e-tailer that __________________. 虚拟零售商最适合被描述为 __________________ 的电子零售商。

Explanation: Virtual merchants are pure-play online retailers who do not own or run physical brick-and-mortar storefronts. 解析：虚拟零售商是纯在线零售商，不拥有或运营实体零售店面。

2. Which of the following is a classic bricks-and-clicks retailer? 以下哪项是经典的实体与网络（Bricks-and-clicks）零售商？

Explanation: Walmart operates massive physical retail stores alongside a comprehensive online web shop, representing a bricks-and-clicks model. 解析：沃尔玛在运营大型实体零售店的同时，还拥有一个综合性的在线网络商城，代表了实体与网络相结合的模式。

3. What does a dropshipping inventory strategy involve? 直运（Dropshipping）库存策略涉及什么？

Explanation: Dropshipping allows e-tailers to sell products without holding physical inventory; orders are shipped directly by third-party suppliers. 解析：直运允许电子零售商在不持有实物库存的情况下销售产品；订单由第三方供应商直接配送。

4. Which financial metric evaluates a firm's total revenue minus cost of goods sold? 哪项财务指标评估企业的总收入减去所售商品成本？

Explanation: Gross profit is calculated by subtracting the cost of goods sold (COGS) from net sales revenue. 解析：毛利润是通过从净销售收入中减去所售商品成本 (COGS) 计算得出的。

5. E-tailing business models create value primarily by __________________. 电子零售商业模式主要通过 __________________ 创造价值。

Explanation: E-tailers provide value through shopping convenience, lower transaction costs, search tools, and massive catalog selections. 解析：电子零售商通过购物便利性、更低的交易成本、搜索工具和庞大的目录选择来提供价值。

6. What is the primary objective of WordPress database tables like wp_wc_orders? WordPress 数据库表如 wp_wc_orders 的主要目标是什么？

Explanation: The wp_wc_orders database table stores all purchase order details, customer addresses, and statuses in WooCommerce. 解析：wp_wc_orders 数据库表存储 WooCommerce 中的所有采购订单详情、客户地址和状态。

7. Analyzing a firm's financial viability involves examining which of the following? 分析企业的财务可行性涉及检查以下哪项？

Explanation: Financial viability analysis audits balance sheets, cash flows, and revenues to verify long-term survivability. 解析：财务可行性分析对资产负债表、现金流量表和收入进行审计，以验证长期的生存能力。

8. Omni-channel retailing refers to: 全渠道零售是指：

Explanation: Omni-channel retailing provides a unified customer shopping experience across online web, mobile app, and physical store touchpoints. 解析：全渠道零售在在线网页、移动应用和实体店触点上为客户提供统一的购物体验。

Revision Review Q&A 课程复习问答

Review Exercise 1 复习练习题 1 ▶

Explain how virtual merchants differ from bricks-and-clicks merchants in terms of cost structure and operational risk. 解释虚拟零售商与实体加鼠标（双渠道）零售商在成本结构和运营风险方面的差异。

1. Cost Structure (成本结构): Virtual merchants have low capital expenditures since they require no brick-and-mortar storefronts, lowering lease and offline staff costs. However, they face high digital customer acquisition costs (CPC marketing). Bricks-and-clicks merchants face high fixed overhead costs (physical leases, utilities, instore inventory management).
2. Operational Risk (运营风险): Virtual merchants face inventory challenges (relying on third-party dropshippers) and low barriers to entry from competitors. Bricks-and-clicks face inventory obsolescence risks and channel conflict (web sales competing with franchise store sales). 1. 成本结构：虚拟零售商的资本支出较低，因为他们不需要实体店面，从而降低了租金和线下员工成本。然而，他们面临高昂的数字客户获取成本（CPC 营销）。实体加鼠标零售商面临高昂的固定间接成本（物理租赁、公用事业、店内库存管理）。
2. 运营风险：虚拟零售商面临库存挑战（依赖第三方代发货商）和竞争对手的低准入门槛。实体加鼠标零售商面临库存过时风险和渠道冲突（网络销售与加盟店销售竞争）。
